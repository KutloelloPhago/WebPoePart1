When it started

She Leads was born out of a vision for a more inclusive and empowered future. On the 9th of August 2021, our journey began at the University of the Witwatersrand in South Africa, as an initiative led by the former SRC Deputy President, Sthembiso Dabula.

Empowerment Through Action

At She Leads, we believe that true change comes from empowerment. We provide a platform for those who have been empowered to pay it forward. Through seminars, workshops, and conferences, we aim to create spaces for meaningful conversations and growth.

Reaching Every Corner

We understand the importance of inclusivity. That's why we take our initiatives to poverty-stricken areas and communities facing severe deprivations. We believe that empowerment should know no boundaries.

Nurturing Future Leaders

She Leads is devoted to cultivating the leaders of the future, with a special emphasis on young women in institutions of higher learning. Through targeted leadership training programs, we empower them with the skills essential for success. Our collaborations with the private and public sectors open doors to a spectrum of opportunities, providing holistic support from career development to economic empowerment.

Join us in our mission to foster a generation of empowered and visionary leaders.
